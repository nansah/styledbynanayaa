[ Official Press Bio ]

Nana Yaa Ansah

Nana Yaa Ansah is a New York City-based fashion, faith, and lifestyle creator whose platform sits at the intersection of high style and intentional living. Known for her editorial eye and authentic storytelling, she has built a loyal community of 150,000+ followers across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Pinterest.

A first-generation creative, Nana Yaa's work bridges the gap between luxury fashion and everyday life — celebrating beauty, purpose, and faith as inseparable forces. Her content blends runway-level aesthetics with candid personal reflection, earning her recognition as one of New York's most distinctive voices in the fashion and wellness space.

She has collaborated with global brands across fashion, beauty, and lifestyle categories, and is regularly featured at New York Fashion Week, brand activations, and industry events throughout the city.
